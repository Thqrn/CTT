## apps

# useful apps, a lot of them are hosted on [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org)

* [Firefox](https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/browsers/mobile/android/): supports a ton of [browser extensions](../misc/browser-extensions.md)
* [F-droidify](https://f-droid.org/packages/com.looker.droidify/): better f-droid frontend
* [Aurora Store](https://f-droid.org/packages/com.aurora.store/): better google play store frontend
* [Aves Libre](https://f-droid.org/packages/deckers.thibault.aves.libre/): heavenly good image gallery, with excellent tag searching/filtering
* [Revanced](https://revanced.app/): patched youtube app (adblock, sponsorblock, dearrow, hide shorts, TONs of customization), watch for fake sites, its `revanced.app`
    * [Tubular](https://f-droid.org/packages/org.polymorphicshade.tubular/): A [NewPipe](https://f-droid.org/packages/org.schabi.newpipe/) fork also works as a Soundcloud frontend, no ads and sponsorblock as well 
* [syncthing-fork](https://f-droid.org/packages/com.github.catfriend1.syncthingandroid/): useful to sync obsidian notes!
* [Obtainium](https://obtainium.imranr.dev/): install and update apps directly from sources like GitHub Releases
* [Musicolet](https://krosbits.in/musicolet/): offline music player, customizable 
* [Fossify](https://www.fossify.org/apps/): simple apps
* [OpenPods](https://f-droid.org/packages/com.dosse.airpods/): AirPods battery at a glance
* [p!n](https://f-droid.org/packages/de.nproth.pin/): pin todos and reminders as notifications with expiry
* [Thunderbird](https://f-droid.org/packages/net.thunderbird.android): formally K-9, a mail client
* [Organic Maps](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/app.organicmaps/): [Open Street Map](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/app.organicmaps/) frontend that makes google maps-style itineraries
* [Parsec](https://parsec.app/downloads): supports plugging in a keyboard, plug your phone with it into a monitor, connect to your remote pc and have ultimate portability :D
